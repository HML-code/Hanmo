What is Photography

Photography is derived from the Greek words "phōtós" (light) and "graphé" (drawing), meaning "drawing with light." It involves capturing images through a camera, which focuses light reflected from objects onto a light-sensitive surface, creating a photograph. This process can be done using traditional film or modern digital sensors.
